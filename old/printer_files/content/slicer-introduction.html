PrusaSlicer

PrusaSlicer is the software we use to turn digital designs into physical 3D printed objects. It takes a 3D model created on a computer and breaks it into hundreds or thousands of thin layers, or “slices.” These slices are then converted into instructions, called G-code, that the printer follows to build the object layer by layer.

In this section, you will learn how to use the essential features of PrusaSlicer and how to handle the most common situations you’ll encounter when preparing a model for printing. Developing these skills will help you avoid failed prints, reduce material waste, and produce higher-quality parts.

Prusa MK4S 3D Printers

3D printing, also known as additive manufacturing, is a process of constructing physical objects from a digital file made using computer-aided design (CAD) software or a 3D scanner. Unlike traditional subtractive manufacturing, which removes material from a block, 3D printing builds objects layer by layer, depositing materials such as plastics, metals, or resins until the final object is complete.

You will primarily have access to Prusa MK4S printers in our makerspace. The main goal of this section is to make the usage of these printers as simple, streamlined, and accessible as possible.

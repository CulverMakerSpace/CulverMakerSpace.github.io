Welcome to Pigott Center for Advanced Technology and Design!

This is the main site for all of the training material for the Culver Academies makerspace.

The training information on this site will teach you how to safely and effectively use the tools in the makerspace, including: CAD software, 3D printers, laser cutters and engravers, and our vinyl cutter.

Each section includes multiple lessons with guided instruction, examples, and quick checks to ensure you understand the material before moving forward.

At the end of each lesson you will need to conduct a practical demonstration of your mastery of the material from that lesson, and at the end of the section there is a short challenge to show your mastery of what you learned.

How to Use This Training

  • Go to one of the training sections using the buttons along the top of the page.
  • Use the navigation on the left to move through each section.
  • Work through pages in order.
  • Watch all videos and review examples carefully.
  • Complete the quick check questions at the bottom of each page.
  • Complete the practical demonstration at the end of each lesson and the challenge at the end of the section.

Your progress will be tracked as you complete each section. Once finished, you will be cleared to use the equipment independently.

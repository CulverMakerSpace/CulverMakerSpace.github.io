This is an external course that was developed by Autodesk. Learn Fusion for CAD in 90 Minutes is a fast-paced, hands-on introduction for new users that builds core CAD skills through a guided bike pedal design project, covering part modeling, assemblies, drawings, and rendering through practical, project-based learning.
